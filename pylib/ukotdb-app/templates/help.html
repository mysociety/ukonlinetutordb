Help

Hopefully this site is easy enough to use that you don't need a help section for the most frequent tasks. However there are some bits that are trickier and so documented here.

What are the different user levels?

There are three user levels - Tutors, Centre Managers and Head Office staff.

Tutors

Tutors are associated to one or more centres and are able to see and update their own information, and create certificates for their students.

They cannot see the details of others users.

Centre Managers

A user can be marked as a 'Centre Manager' for specific centres. They have the same abilities as a Tutor. In addition to they are allowed to see the contact details and certificates of all other tutors for their centre.

Centre Managers cannot change the contact details of other users.

Head Office staff

Head Office staff have full access to see and edit almost anything on the site. This allows them to promote Tutors to be Centre Managers. They are also able to update the contact details for a particular user.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change a Tutor to be a Centre Manager

Only the Head Office Staff can do this. Have the person create an account and add themselves to the centre as a tutor. Once this is done use the Full Admin to list all the 'Tenures'. Find the appropriate one and change the 'role' to 'Centre Manager' and save the changes.

How do I make someone Head Office

Only Head Office staff can do this. Have them create an account. They do not need to belong to a centre. Find them under 'Tutors' in the admin and enable the 'Staff status' checkbox (so that they can log in to the admin area) and also highlight the 'Head Office' group (so that they may make changes).
